About

Carsten Baars

FX TD and Procedural Systems Artist based in Germany, specializing in large-scale simulations and tool development for feature films, episodic series and high-end commercials.

With 8+ years in production VFX, I bridge the gap between technical rigor and artistic vision. I build systems that scale, simulations that hold up under the tightest scrutiny, and pipelines that don't collapse at 3AM the night before a delivery.

My workflow is built around Houdini as the core of everything — from look dev prototypes through final cache delivery. I write VEX and Python daily, develop reusable HDAs, and thrive in the intersection between art and engineering.

Currently open to freelance FX work and full-time FX TD / Pipeline TD positions worldwide.
